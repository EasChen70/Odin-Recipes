Dumplings

Ingredients

  • Ground pork
  • Dumpling wrappers
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Ginger (minced)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Green onions (chopped)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe Instructions

  1. Combine ground pork, ginger, garlic, green onions, soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Spoon filling onto dumpling wrappers
  3. Moisten edges, fold in half, and press to seal.
  4. Steam for 10-12 mins or pan-fry until golden, then steam with water.
  5. Enjoy with soy sauce!
