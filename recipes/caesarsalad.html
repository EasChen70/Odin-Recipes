Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce (chopped)
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Caesar dressing

Recipe Instructions

  1. Mix chopped Romaine lettuce, croutons, and grated Parmesan in a bowl.
  2. Drizzle Caesar dressing over the salad.
  3. Toss the salad to coat evenly and serve.
  4. Enjoy! Feel free to add Chicken or Salmon if you want extra protein!
