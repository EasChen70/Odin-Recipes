Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Bacon or pancetta (diced)
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Black Pepper

Recipe Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti until al dente.
  2. In a pan, cook diced bacon until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and grated Parmesan in a bowl.
  4. Mix cooked pasta with bacon, then add the egg-cheese mixture.
  5. Sprinkle freshly ground black pepper.
  6. Enjoy!
